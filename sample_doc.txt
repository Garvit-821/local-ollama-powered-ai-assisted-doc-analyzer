KITCHEN SCALE WEIGHTS (WEIGH RAW BEFORE COOKING)
• Raw Dals (Chana / Toor / Moong): 50g Raw dry weight = ~130g–150g thick boiled dal.
• Raw Rajma / Chickpeas / Kala Chana: 50g Raw dry weight = ~120g–130g boiled.
• Raw Basmati Rice: 75g Raw dry weight = ~200g boiled rice.
• Chicken / Paneer: Weigh raw before cooking.

DIET EXECUTION RULES
1. Core Maintenance: Carbs are strictly locked at 160g–170g to reload muscle glycogen without fat spillover.
2. Tuesday Protocol: Combine the Morning Shake + Breakfast Option 2/3 + Lunch Combo 2/3 + Snack Option 2
+ Dinner Option 2 to keep things strictly vegetarian.
3. Budget Saving: Keep loose raw dals, besan, eggs, and peanuts as your foundational items to save costs.
